Real Estate Management Application

Welcome to our real estate management application. This platform facilitates the smooth selling of properties by connecting sellers and potential buyers. Our application is built using modern technologies like C#, .Net and Angular for a seamless and interactive experience.

User Roles

Administrator

Responsible for overall management and maintenance of the application.

Seller/Client

Can present real estate to potential buyers, manage their real estate catalogs, and send invitations to clients via email to showcase their properties.

Guest/Customer

Guests can explore real estate catalogs, view property images and descriptions, and contact sellers directly regarding specific properties.
